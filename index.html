Pursuit Monkey

For entrepreneurs just starting out and in the process of determining whether a pursuit will be profitable. We scraped Amazon, Etsy, and Google with Beautiful Soup/Selenium to provide a local, national, and world over view. Equiped with a map of competitors, revenue, and the current state of the market, our goal is to provide concise data and promote local busnisses.
